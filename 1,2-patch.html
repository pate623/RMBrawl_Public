26 June 2018

RM Brawl 1.2 Patch Notes

Big update to resources and early Bronze plays.
Nobility gives movement speed to all fast units, but all of the fast units have decreased base speed.
Both land and water fishing is faster.


Change-logs have been updated to match patch 1.2

Check this to see what all has changed since AOE Rise of Rome 1.0a

Check this to see what all has changed since UPatch 1.1 R4

Villagers and resources

Maps have fewer shore fishes but fisher work rate have been increased to the same ratio with the original game.
Villagers defensive upgrades costs have been reduced, Jihad carry penalty is lower.

Akinaka

Gold Cost: 80 -> 30

Food Cost: 140 -> 80

Research time: 50 -> 30


Jihad

Carry capacity penalty Gold Miner, Stone Miner, Woodcutter:-7 -> -6

Research time: 60 -> 40

Lion/Alligator

Food amount 100 -> 130

Resource decay rate: 1.0 -> 0.4


Shore fish

Amount on maps: Lowered to original values


Fisherman

Work rate: 0.55 -> 0.6


Fish whale

Effective food amount: 250 -> 300

Work rate: 0.4 -> 0.48


Inland

Resource removed: Fish whale
