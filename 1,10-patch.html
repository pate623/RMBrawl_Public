7 Oct 2019

RM Brawl 1.10 Patch Notes

Giving villagers some more base health making them tankier at Tool age but removing the Bronze Age villager technology.
Towers are now stronger.
Slingers were still too strong. Now they are lesser unit on their own but can work well in conjunction with Axemen.
Much more reverts to match AOE:DE.
Now this mod is also available for the AOE DE Steam (version 28529).
